What is Web science?

The Web Science Centre for Doctoral Training, within University of Southampton, defines Web science as the study of the “world’s largest and most complex engineered environment”, including its impact and the changes it brings on human society. Web science is a cross & multi-disciplinary area involving subjects from social sciences, reaching a rage of technical fields of study.
